# 🌟 DiaHelper - AI-Powered Diabetes Risk Assessment

**DiaHelper** is an intelligent web application designed to provide users with a personalized and insightful assessment of their diabetes risk.  
By leveraging a **predictive machine learning model** and **generative AI**, DiaHelper offers actionable health insights, nutritional analysis, and a suite of tools to empower users on their journey toward better health.

## 🚀 Key Features  

### 🧠 AI-Powered Risk Assessment  
Input your health metrics — BMI, glucose levels, age, and more — to receive a **simulated diabetes risk score** generated by a trained predictive model.

### 📄 Personalized AI Reports  
Get comprehensive, easy-to-understand **AI-generated reports** explaining key risk factors and their impact on your health.

### 💬 Interactive Chatbot  
An AI chatbot is available 24/7 to answer your health questions, explain reports, and provide motivational guidance.

### 🥗 Meal Analyzer  
Upload a photo of your meal, and the AI will analyze its **nutritional content** (calories, carbs, fats, etc.) and offer **personalized dietary feedback**.

### 📈 Progress Tracking & History  
Track your assessments over time with **intuitive graphs** showing changes in your diabetes risk, BMI, glucose levels, and other metrics.

### 📓 Health Log  
Maintain a **daily health diary** to log your meals, exercise, medications, and mood. Monitor lifestyle patterns and health progress effortlessly.

### 🔬 Dataset Analyzer  
Upload a CSV dataset of your health metrics to get **batch predictions and aggregated insights**, ideal for research and data enthusiasts.

### 🔒 Secure Authentication  
Built using **Firebase Authentication** to ensure privacy, integrity, and secure data access.

## 🧩 New in DiaHelper v2.0  

### 🧾 1. Prescription Reader *(New Feature)*  
Easily upload your **doctor’s prescription (PDF or image)** and let the AI automatically:
- Extract medicine names, dosage, and schedule.  
- Provide intelligent reminders or alerts for your medications.  
- Offer educational details about the prescribed drugs.  

### 🍚 2. Expanded Health Log with Glycemic Index (GI)  
Now, when users log their meals in the Health Log:
- The AI provides an **estimated Glycemic Index (GI)** for the meal.  
- Explains **how it may impact blood sugar levels**.  
- Suggests alternative food options for **better glycemic control**.  

💡 *Why it matters:* This enhancement helps users understand how different foods affect their blood sugar, aligning perfectly with DiaHelper’s educational and preventive health goals.

### 🔐 3. Improved Logout Functionality  
The logout system has been optimized for **faster session termination**, **enhanced security**, and **smoother navigation** after sign-out.  
- Fixed token persistence bugs.  
- Improved redirection to the login screen.  
- Added visual feedback confirming successful logout.

## 🧠 Machine Learning Model  

The diabetes risk prediction model is trained on standard health datasets (like the **Pima Indians Diabetes Database**).  
It predicts the likelihood of diabetes based on parameters like:
- Glucose Level  
- BMI  
- Blood Pressure  
- Age  
- Insulin  
- Gender
